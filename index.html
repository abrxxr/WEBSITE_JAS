What We Make

Decorative labels built for shoes, garments, accessories, and export-quality product lines.

Heat transfer stickers are polyurethane-based decorative elements applied with heat and pressure. They help brands add sharp graphics, soft-feel labels, stretchable details, and durable finishes while keeping production fast and cost-effective.
